## Impact Assessment
- **Estimated amputee population in Eastern KY:** ~3,000-5,000
- **Nearest prosthetic providers:** Likely in Charleston, WV (~80 mi) or Lexington, KY (~120 mi)
- **Travel burden:** 2+ hours each way; mountain roads make winter travel dangerous
- **Disproportionate impact:** Coal mining-related injuries historically caused high amputation rates; Appalachian diabetes rates exceed national average

## Recommended Actions
1. Establish an O&P satellite practice in Pikeville or Hazard, KY
2. Deploy mobile prosthetic fitting units covering the coalfield region
3. Partner with University of Kentucky or Appalachian Regional Healthcare for telehealth socket fitting
4. Advocate for Kentucky Medicaid to cover travel costs for prosthetic care
5. Leverage the MCHC (Mountain Comprehensive Health Corporation) network for community outreach
