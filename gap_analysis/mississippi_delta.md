## Impact Assessment
- **Estimated amputee population in MS Delta:** ~5,000-8,000
- **Nearest prosthetic providers:** Likely in Memphis, TN (~90 mi) or Jackson, MS (~130 mi)
- **Travel burden:** 1.5-3 hours each way; Delta road conditions and limited public transit exacerbate access barriers
- **Disproportionate impact:** Mississippi has the highest diabetes prevalence in the U.S. (~15%); diabetes-related amputations are 3-4x higher in the Delta than national average; poverty rate exceeds 25%

## Recommended Actions
1. Establish an O&P clinic in Greenville or Indianola, MS
2. Partner with Methodist NeuroRehab in Memphis for telehealth consultations
3. Deploy a mobile prosthetic unit serving the Delta region (Greenville, Indianola, Cleveland, Leland)
4. Advocate for Mississippi Medicaid to cover prosthetic services and travel reimbursement
5. Partner with the Mississippi Limb Loss Amputee Network for peer support and outreach
6. Explore FQHC (Federally Qualified Health Center) partnerships for integrated O&P services
