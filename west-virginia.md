## Key Demographics & Risk Factors

- **West Virginia amputation rate**: ~5.5 per 1,000 adults (above national average)
- **Diabetes prevalence**: 14.7% (highest in the nation) → high amputation risk
- **Rural percentage**: 61% of WV population is rural
- **Poverty rate**: 16% (Raleigh County: 22%)
- **Opioid crisis impact**: IV drug use → increased infection-related amputations

## Recommended Interventions

1. **Mobile prosthetic/orthotic clinic** — Route: Beckley → Oak Hill → Sutton → Princeton → Beckley
2. **Telehealth prosthetics consult line** — Connect rural WV amputees to WVU or VA prosthetics specialists
3. **Community health worker training** — Train local workers in basic stump care and prosthesis maintenance
