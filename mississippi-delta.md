## Key Demographics & Risk Factors

- **Mississippi = highest diabetes prevalence in the U.S.** (15.1%) → highest per-capita amputation rate in the South
- **Racial disparity**: Black amputation rates are 2-3× the national average
- **Poverty rate**: Delta counties among the poorest in the US (~30% below poverty line)
- **Hospital closures**: Multiple Delta hospitals have closed (Kepler Medical Center, Concordia Hospital)
- **No rural transit**: Delta residents have limited options; reliance on personal vehicles
- **MS Delta tornado (2023)**: Rolling Fork devastated; hospital destroyed; no replacement built
